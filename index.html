Safety Tricks & Best Practices

Password Hygiene

Use a unique passphrase for every account. Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) everywhere possible.

Spotting Phishing

Always check the sender's email address. Scammers often use slightly misspelled domains like support@amaz0n.com.

Vishing Protection

Banks will never ask for your PIN over the phone. If in doubt, hang up and call the official number on your bank card.

Link Verification

Hover over links before clicking to see the actual destination URL in the bottom corner of your browser.
